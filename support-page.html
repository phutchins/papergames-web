Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I start a new game?
A: Tap on any game tile from the main screen to begin playing.
Q: Can I play offline?
A: Yes! Paper Games works completely offline - no internet connection required.
Q: How do I play with friends?
A: Select "Local Multiplayer" when starting a game to play with friends on the same device.

Premium Features

Q: What does the Premium upgrade include?
A: Premium removes ads, unlocks full-size game boards, and provides early access to new games.
Q: How do I upgrade to Premium?
A: Tap the "UPGRADE" button on the main screen or go to Settings > Premium.
Q: Can I restore my Premium purchase?
A: Yes, tap "Restore Purchases" in the Premium upgrade screen.

Technical Support

Q: The app is not responding or crashing
A: Try these steps:
  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Update to the latest iOS version
  4. If issues persist, contact us with your device model and iOS version
Q: I can't hear sound effects
A: Check that your device volume is turned up and that the app isn't muted in your device's Control Center.
Q: The app won't load
A: Ensure you have iOS 15.0 or later. Try deleting and reinstalling the app.

Game Rules

Tic-Tac-Toe

  • Get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Take turns placing X's and O's
  • First to get three in a row wins!

Dots & Boxes

  • Connect dots to form lines
  • Complete a box to claim it and score a point
  • Player with the most boxes wins
  • Completing a box gives you another turn
